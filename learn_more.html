PERSONAL LIFE

While trying to find an up and coming singer, Scooter Braun, a former marketing executive of So So Def Recordings, accidently clicked on one of Justin Bieber's 2007 YouTube videos. Braun contacted Bieber's mother who then let 13-year-old Justin Bieber go to Atlanta, GA to begin recording demo tapes.

Bieber was soon signed to Braun and Usher's joint label Raymond Braun Media Group (RBMG) and later signed to Island Records. As he began realeasing music, he quickly rose to fame, with several songs reaching the Billboard Top 100.

Justin Bieber's music is a mix of pop and R&B. His songs are often about his life experiences with heartaches, adolescence, and love. He is known for his "affectedly breathy voice," a style central to all his most popular songs.

Bieber's music and style was influenced by several artists before him, including Michael Jackson, Chris Brown, Justin Timberlake, Stevie Wonder, Mariah Carey, Tupac, and Usher. His music has influenced other artists after him, including Niall Horan of One Direction, Billie Eilish, Charlie Puth, and Why Don't We.

Despite living in the United States, Justin Bieber remains a Canadian citizen. He is married to American model Hailey Rhode Baldwin. The two have been together since November 2018 and currently reside in Beverly Hills, CA.

Prior to meeting Baldwin, Bieber dated American singer Selena Gomez from 2010 to 2018. In an interview on The Ellen Show, Bieber stated that his relationship with Gomez was the inspiration for several of his songs including Sorry, Mark My Words, and What Do You Mean?.
